    About Form Actions

    Form actions are triggered whenever a user interacts with form submission data. There are many types of Form Actions that can be triggered such as saving to a database, sending an email or sending the data to a third party system. You can configure multiple actions on a form as well as make those actions conditional or only trigger for certain actions or at certain points of the process.

    More Info

    It is also possible to intercept the request programatically with Remote Middleware.
