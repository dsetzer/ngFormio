One of the major benefits with building a Serverless Application with Form.io, is the amount of flexibility you have in terms of launching and hosting your application. Some of the more common deployments are as follows.

  • Launch a simple hosted form for quick data collection.
  • Host your web application within a Content Delivery Network (CDN)
  • Compile your application as a native mobile application.
  • Install and run your application on your Local machine.

To read more about how to compile your application for deployment, read our Deploying your Application section of our Help Docs.
